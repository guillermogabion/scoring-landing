The Algorithmic Scoring Engine

Mathematical correctness you can rely on. ScoreFlow eliminates spreadsheet corruption or floating-point anomalies through normalized grading computations.

Deterministic Criteria Normalization

Raw inputs across contrasting scaling bounds are programmatically mapped through a rigid multi-tier computation stack to guarantee clean output weighting:

1. Weighted Multiplier: weighted_score = (score / maxScore) × 100 × (weight / 100)
2. Terminal Summation: judge_total = sum(weighted_scores) across criteria
3. Global Normalization: final_score = average(judge_totals)

Leaderboards are sorted deterministically by descendancy of the resulting structural final_score, assuring high precision accuracy.
